# SEO Audit — nichita-r.com — 2026-07-05

Audited the local prerendered `build/` (current code) across 4 dimensions via parallel agents, cross-checked severe findings against the live production site. Overall: content/titles/descriptions are in good shape, but several **code-level defects in `src/lib/modules/seo.ts` break structured data and social previews site-wide** — high ROI, low effort to fix.

- **M5 sitemap** ☑ — `0` `blocked` entries; trailing slashes now match canonicals; `--reset-time` stamps lastmod.

- **M8 perf** ☑ — hero image `priority` (eager+fetchpriority); `preconnect`/`dns-prefetch` to `imagedelivery.net`. **L1 manifest** ☑ — name/short_name/theme_color fixed.
